Son Tung M-TP About
edit
Son Tung M-TP was born on July 5, 1994 (age 26) in Thai Binh, Vietnam. He is a celebrity pop singer. His full name is Nguyen Thanh Tung. His the best movie is Dandelion. His successful albums are Buong Doi Tay Nhau Ra, Chúng ta không thuộc về nhau, m-tp M-TP. His popular songs are Chạy Ngay Đi2018, Lạc Trôi2017 and Nơi Này Có Anh2017. In His career, Son Tung M-TP has achieved an Dedication Music Award for Best Singer of the Year.
